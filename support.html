Before you contact us

Translation looks wrong — Machine translation can misread context, names, and nuance. For important documents, use a qualified human translator.

“API” or network errors — Check your connection, try again after a few minutes, and update to the latest version of the app or clear site data if you use the web version.

Limits / subscription — Free tiers may cap daily usage. Paid access is managed by the payment provider (for example Stripe); use the receipt link or customer portal to view or cancel.

Billing and subscriptions

For payment receipts, refunds where eligible, and subscription status, use the tools provided by the checkout provider shown at purchase time. GLB does not handle card data directly.

Cancel subscription (self-service, no support email needed)

1) Open Subscription management / cancel

2) Sign in with the email used at purchase

3) Choose Cancel plan

This flow is available anytime. Cancellation and payment-method updates are handled directly in Stripe Customer Portal.

Privacy requests

For privacy-related requests (access, deletion where applicable), email the address used for your account or purchase, or use the same channel you used to buy GLB. Include enough detail to verify your request without sending unnecessary ID.

Bug reports

When reporting a problem, include: device and browser (or app version), steps to reproduce, and a screenshot if possible. Do not attach confidential documents.

Abuse and security

To report abuse of the service or a security issue, contact the operator through the official distribution channel (app store listing or verified project page). Do not publish exploit details publicly before a fix window when reasonable.

Response times

We aim to respond to reasonable inquiries within several business days. Complex legal or regulatory requests may take longer.
